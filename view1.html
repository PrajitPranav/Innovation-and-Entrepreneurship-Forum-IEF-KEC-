Startup Guide

What is a Startup?

A startup is a newly established business designed to scale quickly by offering innovative products or services. Unlike traditional businesses, startups often focus on disrupting markets and leveraging technology.

1. Identify a Problem , Validate Your Idea

Find a real-world problem that people face. Conduct market research using surveys, interviews, and competitor analysis to ensure there is a demand.

2. Develop a Business Model

Use the Business Model Canvas to outline key aspects such as your value proposition, target audience, and revenue streams.

3. Create a Minimum Viable Product (MVP)

Develop a basic version of your product, test with early adopters, and gather feedback to refine your product.

4. Register Your Startup

Choose a business structure (LLC, Corporation, etc.), register your business name, and obtain necessary permits and licenses.

5. Secure Funding

Consider different funding options like angel investors, venture capital, crowdfunding, or government grants.
