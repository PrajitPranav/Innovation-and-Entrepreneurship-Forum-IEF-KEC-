What is the Business Model Canvas?

The Business Model Canvas (BMC) is a strategic tool used to visualize and develop a companyâ€™s business model. It consists of 9 key building blocks that help entrepreneurs and businesses define their value proposition, infrastructure, customers, and revenue streams.

1. Key Partners

Identify the strategic partnerships, suppliers, and stakeholders that help your business operate efficiently.

2. Key Activities

Define the most important tasks your business must perform to create value and run effectively.

3. Key Resources

List the assets (human, financial, physical, intellectual) required to deliver your value proposition.

4. Value Proposition

Describe what makes your product or service unique and why customers should choose you over competitors.

5. Customer Relationships

Define how you interact with customers and maintain a positive relationship (e.g., self-service, personal assistance).

6. Channels

Specify the communication and distribution channels used to reach your customers (e.g., online, physical stores).

7. Customer Segments

Identify your target audience and classify them based on demographics, behavior, or needs.

8. Cost Structure

Analyze the costs associated with running your business, including fixed and variable expenses.

9. Revenue Streams

List the ways your business generates income, such as product sales, subscriptions, or advertising.
