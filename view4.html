Funding Resources

1. Bootstrapping

Using your own savings or reinvesting revenue to grow your business.

2. Angel Investors

High-net-worth individuals who provide capital in exchange for equity.

3. Venture Capital

Investment firms that provide funding for high-growth startups.

4. Crowdfunding

Platforms like Kickstarter and GoFundMe allow raising funds from the public.

5. Bank Loans

Traditional financing from banks with interest and repayment terms.

6. Government Grants

Non-repayable funds provided by the government to support startups.

7. Business Incubators , Accelerators

Programs that offer funding, mentorship, and resources to startups.
